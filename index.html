Our company believes in the power of technology to revolutionize the way we conduct transactions online. Traditional payment systems rely on trusted third parties, leading to higher costs, limited transaction sizes, and increased risk of fraud. We propose a solution based on cryptographic proof, allowing direct transactions between parties without the need for a middleman. Our secure and innovative payment system ensures that transactions are irreversible, protecting sellers from fraud and buyers from payment uncertainties. Join us in the future of online commerce.

Protocol

Liquidity Pool

1% of every transaction will be injected into the liquidity pool to ensure that there is always enough liquidity to facilitate trades and maintain the value of the pool

Marketing

To support the long-term growth and development of our project, we allocate 1% of every transaction to our marketing wallet. This pool of funds will be used to promote our project and expand our reach to new audiences

Treasury

As part of our commitment to delivering long-term value to our users, we allocate 2% of every transaction to our treasury. This pool of funds can be used for a range of activities, including airdrops, charity, rewards, listings, and manual burns.

Burn

To further enhance the value of our token and increase its scarcity over time, we allocate 5% of every transaction to token burn. reducing the total supply of tokens and increasing the value of the remaining tokens.

Features

  • WPAY P2P Exchange: a decentralized exchange that enables peer-to-peer trading of cryptocurrencies without intermediaries.
  • WPAY Wallet With 2FA: a secure wallet for storing and managing cryptocurrencies, with two-factor authentication for enhanced security.
  • WPAY P2P Launchpad: a platform for launching new projects through a decentralized, community-driven process.
  • WPAY Blockchain: decentralized blockchain network with smart contract functionality, providing a fast and low-cost platform for transactions.
  • WPAY QR Scan: a feature that allows users to scan QR codes for easy cryptocurrency transactions.
  • WPAY Card (Visa, Master card): a debit card that allows users to spend their cryptocurrencies in real-world transactions.
  • WPAY NFT Marketplace: a marketplace for buying and selling non-fungible tokens (NFTs).
  • Token Listings: WPAY is listed on major cryptocurrency tracking websites (Coinmarketcap, Coingecko) and over 100 exchanges, providing greater accessibility and visibility to users
